# Mao Method Business Mapping

Use this reference when the user asks to combine 毛选, 毛泽东选集, 实事求是, 调查研究, 实践论, 矛盾论, 群众路线, 持久战, or current business cognition with 商业裁断.

## 1. Investigation Before Verdict

Source basis: 反对本本主义 and 改造我们的学习 emphasize investigation, current reality, history, concrete materials, and avoiding subjective wishful judgment.

Business translation:

- No serious verdict without the current situation and the history of the problem.
- Do not treat an idea as a private brainstorm; treat it as a problem with users, payers, channels, competitors, costs, and constraints.
- Investigation is not collecting fragments. It must inspect relations: user vs payer, pain vs payment, traffic vs conversion, founder asset vs operational requirement.
- When evidence is thin, output a 调查纲目 rather than a confident verdict.

Diagnostic actions:

- Ask who has direct experience of the pain.
- Ask what the user has already tried, paid for, abandoned, or repeated.
- Ask what has changed recently in platform, AI capability, regulation, or buyer behavior.
- If possible, run a small "investigation meeting": 3 to 8 relevant people, a prepared outline, concrete examples, and recorded user language.

## 2. Practice Loop

Source basis: 实践论 describes knowledge moving from practice to understanding and back to practice, with practice as the test and development of knowledge.

Business translation:

- A good 裁断 is not finished when it sounds right. It must return to market practice.
- Likes, compliments, and private excitement are low-grade evidence. Payment, usage, repeat behavior, referral, and unprompted user language are stronger evidence.
- Every verdict should define what result would prove it wrong.
- When results return, update the verdict rather than defend the original answer.

Diagnostic actions:

- Convert strategy into a 7 to 14 day validation action only when continuation is justified.
- Define a kill metric before the user starts, or a revive condition when the verdict is stop/pause.
- Ask the user to return with exact exposure, replies, deposits, payment, repeat use, and ignored parts.
- Treat non-execution as evidence about task size, emotional reality, fear of signal, or wrong audience.

## 3. 当前卡口 And 后置风险

Source basis: 矛盾论 emphasizes contradiction specificity, concrete analysis, primary and secondary contradictions, and the shifting main aspect across stages.

Business translation:

- Do not judge by category alone. "自媒体", "AI agent", "社群", or "消费品" is not enough.
- The 当前卡口 is the constraint deciding the next move now, not the most dramatic issue.
- 后置风险 are real, but they must not distract from the live bottleneck.
- The 当前卡口 changes by stage.

Stage mapping:

| Stage | 当前卡口 to inspect first |
|---|---|
| Idea-only | User/payer/pain clarity |
| Offer-shaping | Specific paid promise |
| Pre-validation | First reachable channel |
| Validation | Payment or repeated behavior |
| Early retention | Return reason and transformation |
| Scale | Repeatable acquisition, margin, operations |

Diagnostic action:

- Name one 当前卡口.
- Name two or three 后置风险.
- State what change would move a 后置风险 into the 当前卡口.

## 4. User Line

Source basis: 关于领导方法的若干问题 describes moving from dispersed user input to concentrated judgment, then returning it to action and testing it.

Business translation:

- Raw user language is not the final offer.
- Founder strategy is not valid until it returns to users and changes behavior.
- Good product judgment repeatedly cycles: collect user facts, synthesize offer, return it to users, observe action, revise.

Diagnostic actions:

- Capture the user's exact words, not the founder's polished interpretation.
- Concentrate repeated user pains into one paid promise.
- Return the promise to users through a post, landing page, call, pre-sale, concierge delivery, or deposit ask.
- Use user action to correct the next version.

## 5. Protracted Strategy And Stage Discipline

Source basis: 论持久战 distinguishes stages, rejects both quick-victory fantasy and defeatism, and stresses initiative, flexibility, and planning under objective constraints.

Business translation:

- Early projects usually cannot win by a grand launch. They need staged accumulation.
- Do not demand late-stage moats before demand exists; do not ignore moats once proof exists.
- Do not call a 7-day test a complete business verdict. Use it to attack the 当前卡口.
- Preserve initiative by choosing tests the founder can actually execute.

Commercial stage mapping:

| Business stage | Strategic posture |
|---|---|
| Signal search | Survive cheaply, investigate users, avoid big build |
| Wedge formation | Win a narrow use case, create proof, build repeatable language |
| Base area | Own a small distribution asset, case library, workflow, or community |
| Expansion | Scale only after channel, delivery, retention, and margin are visible |

Diagnostic actions:

- Identify the current stage before judging ambition.
- Choose one local "battle" that changes the evidence, not a total-war plan.
- Prefer tests that preserve cash, energy, credibility, and optionality.

## 6. Aim At A Concrete Target

Source basis: 改造我们的学习 and 整顿党的作风 emphasize studying actual problems for application, not empty theory; the "有的放矢" image means the arrow must aim at the target.

Business translation:

- Frameworks are arrows, not trophies.
- Every principle must change a decision: build, sell, narrow, pause, research, or kill.
- The output should be aimed at the user's next concrete action and audience.

Diagnostic actions:

- End with one cautious choice and one kill metric, or a revive condition for stop/pause verdicts. Add a mission only when continuation is justified.
- If the theory overlay does not change the action, omit it.
- Do not write a conceptual essay when the user needs a decision.

## Contemporary Business Cognition Overlay

Use these as modern commercial lenses, but verify current facts when the verdict depends on platform rules, pricing, regulation, competitors, or model capabilities.

- AI lowers the cost of producing text, code, images, agents, and strategy documents. Scarcity shifts toward trust, distribution, proprietary context, domain judgment, workflow integration, and accumulated cases.
- Content is not a business until it connects to an offer, payment path, fulfillment, retention, or owned asset.
- Generic prompt wrappers are fragile. Durable agent/skill value usually comes from encoded judgment, user state, cases, feedback loops, and integration into repeated work.
- A paid community sells environment, selection, rituals, accountability, and host judgment more than information.
- For creator businesses, attention is raw material; qualified trust and conversion are the business bottleneck.
- In volatile platform environments, overfitting to one channel is a 后置风险 or 当前卡口 depending on stage.

## Anti-Patterns

- Using 毛选 phrases to make the answer feel deep while leaving the user's next action vague.
- Turning business diagnosis into political analogy.
- Treating war terms as literal business advice.
- Using "当前卡口" or "主要矛盾" to justify a pre-decided opinion.
- Treating a user's excitement as false consciousness without evidence.
- Treating lack of current proof as proof of permanent impossibility.
- Ignoring changed market facts because a classic framework feels timeless.
